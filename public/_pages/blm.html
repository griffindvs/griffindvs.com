Donations will be split between Reclaim the Block, Black Visions Collective, and Minnesota Freedom Fund.

Minnesota Freedom Fund - minnesotafreedomfund.org

Posts bond for protestors.

Black Lives Matter - blacklivesmatter.com

Works to stop acts of violence inflicted on Black communities.

George Floyd Memorial Fund - gofundme.com/f/georgefloyd

Funds will cover funeral and burial expenses, mental and grief counseling, and lodging and travel for all court proceedings.

Northstar Health Collective - northstarhealthcollective.org

Provides medical help to protestors.

Black Visions Collective - blackvisionsmn.org

Dedicated to supporting Black communities suffering from injustice.

Reclaim the Block - reclaimtheblock.org

Organizes Minneapolis community and city council members to move money from the police department into other areas of the city’s budget that truly promote community health and safety.

NAACP Legal Defense - naacp.org

Works through the legal system to support Black communities.

Washington Post Database of Police Killings

"1,011 people have been killed by police in the past year... Although half of the people shot and killed by police are white, black Americans are shot at a disproportionate rate. They account for less than 13 percent of the U.S. population, but are killed by police at more than twice the rate of white Americans."

How to Be Antiracist
by Ibram X. Kendi

"Racist ideas have defined our society since its beginning and can feel so natural and obvious as to be banal.... To be an antiracist is a radical choice in the face of this history, requiring radical reorientation of our consciousness."

Why I'm No Longer Talking To White People About Race
by Reni Eddo-Lodge

"Eddo-Lodge pushes readers to recognize that racism is a systemic problem that needs to be tackled by those who run the system."

"Police: Sixth-leading cause of death for young black men"

"For young men of color in the United States, police use-of-force is among the leading causes of death, according to a study from the University of Michigan, Rutgers University and Washington University."

"Racial and Ethnic Disparities Continue in Pregnancy-Related Deaths"

"Black, American Indian, and Alaska Native (AI/AN) women are two to three times more likely to die from pregnancy-related causes than white women – and this disparity increases with age, researchers from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) report..."

"Race and Economic Opportunity in the United States"
from the Equality of Opportunity Project

"Black-white disparities exist in virtually all regions and neighborhoods. Some of the best metro areas for economic mobility for low-income black boys are comparable to the worst metro areas for low-income white boys... And black boys have lower rates of upward mobility than white boys in 99 percent of Census tracts in the country."

The 1619 Project

"The 1619 Project is an ongoing initiative from The New York Times Magazine that began in August 2019, the 400th anniversary of the beginning of American slavery. It aims to reframe the country’s history by placing the consequences of slavery and the contributions of black Americans at the very center of our national narrative."
